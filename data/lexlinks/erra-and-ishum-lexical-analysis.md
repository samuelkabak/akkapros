## Step 2: Identify Nouns in Construct State

A noun in construct state is:

- Immediately followed by another noun (in genitive) or a pronominal
  suffix
- Forms a single stress unit with the following word
- Cannot receive independent stress

- **[F]**: Function word (from list)
- **[C]**: Noun in construct state
- **[S]**: Stressable word (all others)
